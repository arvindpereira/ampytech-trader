Theme narrative

Quantum computing remains a long-duration theme where technical leadership, commercial readiness and investability are not the same thing. IBM can plausibly rank near the top in a technology-readiness or developer-ecosystem framework because of Qiskit, superconducting systems and enterprise access, which is what Gemini emphasized. This ranking instead treated the theme as a public-equity basket and rewarded balance-sheet/fundamental quality, broad AI/HPC infrastructure positioning, news tone and momentum; that naturally favors NVDA and GOOGL over several purer but less-covered quantum names. The snapshot also has clear data-quality problems: some entries such as BE, FOR, SEEMS, TO and YOUR appear to be ticker/parser artifacts rather than clean quantum-company selections, so the output should not be read as a clean scientific ranking of quantum capability.
